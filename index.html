Diseases

Leaf Spot

Fungal or bacterial spots on leaves; fungicide may be required.

Powdery Mildew

White powder-like fungal growth on leaves and stems.

Rust

Orange-brown pustules causing leaf damage and reduced vigor.

Blight

Rapid plant tissue damage, often requiring fast intervention.

Root Rot

Root decay caused by poor drainage and pathogen activity.

Bacterial Wilt

Sudden wilting caused by bacterial infection in vascular tissue.

Fusarium Wilt

Soil-borne fungal wilt that blocks plant water movement.

Anthracnose

Dark sunken lesions on leaves, stems, and fruits.

Scab

Rough lesions on fruits or tubers affecting quality.

Mosaic Virus

Patchy leaf discoloration often spread by insect vectors.

Downy Mildew

Leaf yellowing and fungal growth under humid conditions.

Black Spot

Dark circular lesions that reduce photosynthesis area.

Canker

Dead, sunken infected stem or branch regions.

Dieback

Progressive death of shoots and branches from the tip.

Smut

Fungal disease producing dark powdery masses.

Septoria Leaf Blotch

Leaf lesions that reduce green area and vigor.
